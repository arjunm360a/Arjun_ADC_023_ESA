Brief

Make an app solving any issue or developing a business on an app.

Problem Statement

To make public transportation a lot more inclusive for the Visually disabled.

Pain Points

  • Lack of inclusivity for the visually challenged in the public transport sector.
  • Lack of dignity.
  • No proper accessibility integration.
  • No equal opportunity

Solutions

  • SAHAYAK is a revolutionary bus transit app designed specifically for the visually impaired community, empowering users to navigate public transportation with confidence and ease.
  • Intuitive Voice Guidance: SAHAYAK provides clear and precise voice instructions throughout the journey, ensuring users receive real-time updates and directions.
  • Customized Route Planning: Users can plan routes by inputting their destination. The app considers user preferences and accessibility features to provide optimized travel plans.
  • Accessible Bus Stop Information: Detailed information about nearby bus stops, including names, routes, schedules, and accessibility features, helps users find the best stops.
  • Real-Time Bus Tracking: Stay informed about bus arrivals and departures with SAHAYAK's live tracking feature, ensuring users never miss their bus.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The app's interface is designed with large buttons, high contrast colors, and customizable settings, making it easy to navigate for visually impaired users.
