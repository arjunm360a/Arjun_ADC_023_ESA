Problem Statement

Since one of my biggest interests lies in doodling and traveling. I choose to make a brand that shows off those two aspects as best as possible, coming up with a blend like no other of the two activities.

Brand Story

As the sun sets and campfires crackle, friendships are forged, stories are shared, and memories are made. Each stroke of the brush becomes a brushstroke of a larger canvas a canvas that tells the story of Scribble Safari, where creativity knows no bounds and friendships flourish in the magical realm of the enchanted woods.

Typography

I created a playful custom font for my brand, designed with a fun, scribble-like style. It captures the lighthearted and whimsical side of the brand.
