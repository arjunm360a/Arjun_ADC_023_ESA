Brief

Based on an existing game, redesigned to create something new.

Concept

We took up the game Hunters Vs Assassin. We added a time travel concept to the game to get rid of the repetitive loop, making it more open to new concepts and ideas. This enhances engagement and fun for the users.

Old Game Flaws

  • The game suffered from a lack of updates. This was not because the developers didn't care or the game wasn't working but instead the game didn't have a good plot or story to give new content to the users.
  • Lack of assets.
  • No rewards or reason for the players to come back.
Gameplay Screens
Leaderboard: Purpose of a leaderboard is to introduce a competitive nature among the casual players.
More action: By introducing a more action-packed game, the chances of keeping the player hooked and engrossed will increase.
Daily rewards: By giving each player a daily reward you give them a purpose to come back the next day for more.
Weekly Rewards: Awarding players for playing your game for longer than others will only increase your players' return rate.
